● Expression
- An expression is a combination of items, like variables, literals,
operators, and parentheses, that evaluates to a value.

● Operator
- An operator is a symbol that
performs a built-in calculation, like the operator + which performs
addition.

● Evaluate
- form an idea of the amount, number, or value of; assess.

● Precedence
- the order of standars mathematics

● Floating-point
-A floating-point number is a real number, like 
98.6, 0.0001, or -666.667.
